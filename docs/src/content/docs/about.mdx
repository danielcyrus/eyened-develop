The EyeNED Platform was originally designed by the [EyeNED Reading Centre](https://www.eyened.nl)  at Erasmus MC and Radboudumc as an in-house tool for the 
annotation of ophthalmic imaging. Over the years it has increased in complexity and functionality, and in 2025 it became open-source.

At the end of 2025 we were awarded a grant from the Dutch Open Science Infrastructure programme to
build [a national infrastructure for ophthalmic imaging](https://www.nwo.nl/en/projects/ihnbj79142), together with the BIGR infrastructure
group at Erasmus MC and qurAI at the University of Amsterdam. From 2026 to 2030, we will extend the EyeNED Platform with open-source modules
for importing and harmonizing data, build a secure and accessible national cloud service based on the EyeNED Platform, and integrate
this platform with external services for the use of (AI) algorithms. Since 2025, we also partnered with the *Moorfields Reading Centre* 
to jointly develop and maintain the open-source EyeNED Platform.

## How to get involved

Do you want to learn more about the project, did you run into any problems or are you eager to contribute? Please reach out through 
our [Github Discussions](https://github.com/Eyened/eyened-platform/discussions), [make an issue](https://github.com/Eyened/eyened-platform/issues)
or [contact us directly](mailto:eyened@erasmusmc.nl).
